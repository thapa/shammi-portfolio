# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

E-commerce businesses, startups, and brands evaluating a CRO expert and Shopify/WordPress developer. Viewing during vendor research, likely on desktop. Job: quickly assess quality, trust the work, decide to reach out.

## Product Purpose

One-page portfolio that converts visitors into clients by demonstrating expertise in CRO, Shopify, and WordPress development. Success means an inquiry — the design must earn trust and make contacting feel like the obvious next step.

## Brand Personality

Dark, technical, premium. Confident and precise, not flashy. The design itself should signal the same caliber as the work it showcases. Voice is direct and expert — no hedging, no over-explaining.

## References

- sent.dm/en — dark, premium, intentional.

## Anti-references

- Inter font — too generic, ubiquitous, signals no typographic opinion
- Gradient effects — SaaS template reflex, visually noisy
- Multi-color palettes — scattered identity, undermines the premium signal
- Anything that reads as an AI-generated or template portfolio

## Design Principles

1. Precision as personality — the craft in the design reflects the craft in the work
2. Restraint over decoration — nothing decorative; every element earns its place
3. Work over words — portfolio pieces speak louder than descriptive copy
4. One voice — single strong visual identity, no scattered themes or competing aesthetics
5. Convert by confidence — hierarchy and layout guide visitors toward making contact

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA compliance. Standard considerations for contrast on dark backgrounds.
